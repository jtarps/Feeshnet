# Feeshnet Media - Marketing Team Job Descriptions

## Social Media Manager

### About Feeshnet Media
Feeshnet Media is a digital marketing agency specializing in healthcare and service-based businesses. We create authentic, compelling content that helps our clients connect with their audiences and grow their impact.

### About This Role
You'll be managing social media for a home healthcare company that provides compassionate, professional care services helping seniors maintain their independence and quality of life. This is an opportunity to make a real difference by connecting families with the care they need.

### Position Overview
We're seeking a creative and organized Social Media Manager to join our team and build a strong social media presence for our home healthcare client. This remote role is perfect for someone who understands how to create emotional connections through social content and can manage the daily operations of multiple social channels.

### Key Responsibilities

**Content Management & Scheduling**
- Create and maintain a monthly content calendar aligned with client marketing initiatives
- Schedule 4-5 posts per day across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok
- Repurpose content across platforms with platform-specific optimization
- Coordinate with video editor and content writer to ensure cohesive messaging

**Community Engagement**
- Monitor and respond to all comments, messages, and mentions within 24 hours
- Build relationships with followers through authentic, empathetic engagement
- Identify and engage with relevant community groups and conversations
- Flag urgent inquiries or concerns to agency leadership immediately

**Strategy & Analytics**
- Track performance metrics (engagement, reach, follower growth, conversions)
- Provide weekly analytics reports with insights and recommendations
- Stay current on social media trends, especially in healthcare and senior care
- A/B test content types, posting times, and messaging approaches

**Content Creation**
- Design basic social graphics using Canva or similar tools
- Write compelling captions that reflect the brand voice (warm, professional, trustworthy)
- Source and curate relevant third-party content to share
- Create Instagram Stories, Reels, and TikTok videos from provided footage

### Required Skills & Qualifications
- 2-3 years of experience managing social media for brands (healthcare/senior care experience highly valued)
- Strong writing skills with ability to adapt tone for different platforms
- Experience with social media management tools (Hootsuite, Buffer, or similar)
- Basic graphic design skills (Canva proficiency required)
- Understanding of social media analytics and reporting
- Excellent organizational and time management skills
- High emotional intelligence for community management
- Comfortable working in a remote agency environment

### Preferred Qualifications
- Experience in healthcare, senior care, or B2C service industries
- Knowledge of paid social advertising (Facebook Ads Manager)
- Video editing skills for Stories and Reels
- Understanding of accessibility best practices for social content

### Technical Requirements
- Reliable internet connection for remote work
- Proficiency with Meta Business Suite, LinkedIn, TikTok Creator tools
- Comfortable with Google Workspace and project management tools

### Work Schedule
- Full-time remote position (40 hours/week)
- Some flexibility required for real-time engagement and time-sensitive posts
- Availability during US business hours preferred

## Content Writer/Strategist

### About Feeshnet Media
Feeshnet Media is a digital marketing agency specializing in healthcare and service-based businesses. We create authentic, compelling content that helps our clients connect with their audiences and grow their impact.

### About This Role
You'll be creating content for a home healthcare company that provides compassionate, professional care services helping seniors maintain their independence. This is an opportunity to use your writing skills to help families facing difficult care decisions find the support they need.

### Position Overview
We're looking for a talented Content Writer/Strategist to join our team and create compelling, empathetic content for our home healthcare client. This remote role requires someone who can balance emotional storytelling with SEO best practices and clear calls-to-action.

### Key Responsibilities

**Content Creation**
- Write 8-12 blog posts per month (800-1,500 words) optimized for SEO
- Develop website copy, landing pages, and service descriptions
- Create email campaigns (newsletters, nurture sequences, promotional)
- Write social media captions in collaboration with Social Media Manager
- Develop case studies and client success stories from interviews
- Create downloadable resources (guides, checklists, FAQs)

**Content Strategy**
- Research and identify relevant keywords and topics for senior care audience
- Develop content pillars that support client business goals and customer journey
- Create monthly content calendars aligned with client marketing initiatives
- Map content to different stages of the buyer's journey
- Identify content gaps and opportunities through competitive research

**SEO & Optimization**
- Conduct keyword research using tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Ubersuggest
- Optimize all content for search engines while maintaining readability
- Write compelling meta descriptions and title tags
- Update and refresh existing content for improved performance
- Monitor content performance and make data-driven recommendations

**Collaboration**
- Work with agency leadership and client to understand brand voice and positioning
- Interview staff, caregivers, and families for authentic stories
- Provide content briefs to graphic designer for supporting visuals
- Ensure consistency across all written materials

### Required Skills & Qualifications
- 3-5 years of professional content writing experience
- Strong portfolio demonstrating versatile writing across formats
- Proven experience with SEO and keyword optimization
- Excellent research skills with ability to understand complex topics quickly
- Exceptional grammar, spelling, and editing abilities
- Experience writing for healthcare, senior care, or related industries strongly preferred
- Ability to write with empathy and sensitivity about aging and care
- Comfortable working in a remote agency environment with client communication

### Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with WordPress or other CMS platforms
- Familiarity with email marketing platforms (Mailchimp, Constant Contact, HubSpot)
- Understanding of conversion copywriting principles
- Knowledge of ADA compliance and accessible content
- Basic understanding of Google Analytics

### Technical Requirements
- Proficiency with Google Workspace
- Experience with SEO tools (at least basic keyword research)
- Comfortable with content management systems
- Reliable internet connection for remote work

### Work Schedule
- Full-time remote position (40 hours/week)
- Flexible hours with core overlap during US business hours
- Deadline-driven work with predictable monthly workflow

## Graphic Designer

### About Feeshnet Media
Feeshnet Media is a digital marketing agency specializing in healthcare and service-based businesses. We create authentic, compelling content that helps our clients connect with their audiences and grow their impact.

### About This Role
You'll be creating visual content for a home healthcare company that provides compassionate care services to seniors and their families. This is an opportunity to use your design skills to help families discover and connect with quality care services.

### Position Overview
We're seeking a versatile Graphic Designer to join our team and create visual content supporting our home healthcare client's marketing efforts across digital and print channels. This part-time remote role requires someone who can translate a warm, professional brand into compelling visuals that resonate with adult children caring for aging parents.

### Key Responsibilities

**Social Media Graphics**
- Design 15-20 social media graphics per week (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Create templates for recurring post types (quotes, tips, announcements)
- Design Instagram Story and Highlight covers
- Develop carousel posts and infographics for educational content
- Ensure all social graphics are optimized for each platform's specifications

**Marketing Materials**
- Design email newsletter templates and promotional graphics
- Create blog post featured images and in-article graphics
- Develop downloadable resources (PDF guides, checklists, worksheets)
- Design one-pagers, brochures, and print materials as needed
- Create presentation templates for sales and business development

**Brand Consistency**
- Maintain and expand brand style guide with templates and assets
- Ensure visual consistency across all marketing touchpoints
- Organize and maintain digital asset library
- Create brand guidelines for team reference

**Collaboration**
- Work closely with Content Writer and Social Media Manager on the team
- Translate content briefs into visual concepts
- Incorporate feedback efficiently with quick turnaround
- Suggest visual improvements to enhance message delivery
- Communicate effectively in a remote agency environment

### Required Skills & Qualifications
- 2-4 years of professional graphic design experience
- Strong portfolio showcasing social media graphics and digital marketing materials
- Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign)
- Experience with Canva and creating templates for non-designers
- Understanding of design principles (typography, color theory, composition)
- Ability to design for accessibility (contrast, readability, alt text considerations)
- Fast turnaround time with strong attention to detail
- Comfortable working in a remote agency environment with multiple stakeholders

### Preferred Qualifications
- Experience designing for healthcare, senior care, or service industries
- Motion graphics or basic video editing skills (After Effects, Premiere Pro)
- Understanding of print production and file preparation
- Photography editing and retouching skills
- Knowledge of current design trends, especially in healthcare marketing

### Technical Requirements
- Adobe Creative Cloud subscription (or proficiency with alternatives)
- Reliable internet connection for file sharing and collaboration
- Ability to work with Google Drive, Dropbox, or similar platforms
- Access to stock photo resources (we can provide if needed)

### Work Schedule
- Part-time remote position (20 hours/week)
- Flexible hours with 24-48 hour turnaround expectations
- Some availability during US business hours for meetings and quick requests
- Potential to grow to full-time as needs expand
