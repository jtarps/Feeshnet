# Content Writer/Strategist
## Feeshnet Media

### About Feeshnet Media
Feeshnet Media is a digital marketing agency specializing in healthcare and service-based businesses. We create authentic, compelling content that helps our clients connect with their audiences and grow their impact.

### About This Role
You'll be creating content for a home healthcare company that provides compassionate, professional care services helping seniors maintain their independence. This is an opportunity to use your writing skills to help families facing difficult care decisions find the support they need.

### Position Overview
We're looking for a talented Content Writer/Strategist to join our team and create compelling, empathetic content for our home healthcare client. This remote role requires someone who can balance emotional storytelling with SEO best practices and clear calls-to-action.

### Key Responsibilities

**Content Creation**
- Write 8-12 blog posts per month (800-1,500 words) optimized for SEO
- Develop website copy, landing pages, and service descriptions
- Create email campaigns (newsletters, nurture sequences, promotional)
- Write social media captions in collaboration with Social Media Manager
- Develop case studies and client success stories from interviews
- Create downloadable resources (guides, checklists, FAQs)

**Content Strategy**
- Research and identify relevant keywords and topics for senior care audience
- Develop content pillars that support client business goals and customer journey
- Create monthly content calendars aligned with client marketing initiatives
- Map content to different stages of the buyer's journey
- Identify content gaps and opportunities through competitive research

**SEO & Optimization**
- Conduct keyword research using tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Ubersuggest
- Optimize all content for search engines while maintaining readability
- Write compelling meta descriptions and title tags
- Update and refresh existing content for improved performance
- Monitor content performance and make data-driven recommendations

**Collaboration**
- Work with agency leadership and client to understand brand voice and positioning
- Interview staff, caregivers, and families for authentic stories
- Provide content briefs to graphic designer for supporting visuals
- Ensure consistency across all written materials

### Required Skills & Qualifications
- 3-5 years of professional content writing experience
- Strong portfolio demonstrating versatile writing across formats
- Proven experience with SEO and keyword optimization
- Excellent research skills with ability to understand complex topics quickly
- Exceptional grammar, spelling, and editing abilities
- Experience writing for healthcare, senior care, or related industries strongly preferred
- Ability to write with empathy and sensitivity about aging and care
- Comfortable working in a remote agency environment with client communication

### Preferred Qualifications

- Knowledge of ADA compliance and accessible content
- Basic understanding of Google Analytics
